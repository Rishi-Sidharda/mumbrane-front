HOW IT WORKS PAGE
Headline: "From Hire To Production In 30 Days"
The Mumbrane Employee Lifecycle
Step 1: Configure Your Employee
Define role, permissions, priorities, and boundaries. Set which tasks require approval and which can execute autonomously. Customize personality and communication style.
Step 2: Initial Onboarding Discussions
Have conversations with your new digital employee about your company, projects, and goals. They build permanent memory of your context, tools, and preferences.
Step 3: Mission Control Setup
Assign tasks, projects, and priorities through the dashboard. Your employee sees their work queue just like a Trello board—always knows what's next.
Step 4: Autonomous Execution
Employee works on assigned tasks with full context awareness. For governed actions, approval requests route to you. Sandbox mode available for risk-free testing.
Step 5: Progress Discussions
Schedule regular check-ins to review progress, adjust priorities, or course-correct. Every discussion updates their permanent memory and mission control state.
Step 6: Scale & Expand
Add more employees to your workforce. They coordinate automatically on shared projects through inter-agent communication protocols.

Technical Architecture
Permanent Memory Layer
Git-like versioning of every conversation, decision, and execution. Roll back to any previous state. Experiences written after every interaction.
Mission Control (Goal Tracking)
Real-time visibility into active tasks, dependencies, and blockers. Historical record of what was worked on and why.
Project State Manager
Large projects automatically decompose into manageable chunks. Sub-agents coordinate without losing coherence or context.
Governance Engine
Approval workflows, permission boundaries, and sandbox execution. You maintain control while enabling autonomy.
Audit System
Comprehensive logs of every action. Complete transparency for compliance, debugging, and optimization.
